## The five principles every site honors

1. **Outcomes-led, not feature-led.** Lead with proven results / ROAI, not tools or certs.
2. **Human + governed AI agents.** Senior strategy and a data foundation come first; AI agents
   are the *governed delivery mechanism*, never the headline. Never say
   "autonomous agents that replace your team."
3. **ROAI is the lingua franca.** "Return on AI Investment = value delivered / AI cost"
   (≈ 4x example). The same metric appears across all three sites.
4. **Local prime + global power.** Emerge Digital is the Dubai Mainland contracting / local entity;
   FPT (80,000+) and Salesforce are the scale and proof.
5. **ICP = Gulf Government / BFSI / Retail** inside the Vision 2030 (2026–2027) window.
